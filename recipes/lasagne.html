Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams lasagne sheets
  • 500 grams ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce followed by a layer of lasagne sheets. Repeat until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce.
  5. Spread ricotta cheese over the top and sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
