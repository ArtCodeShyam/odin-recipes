Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish with tender chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, ginger, and garlic for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© the onions until golden brown. Add garam masala, chili powder, and turmeric powder, and cook for a minute.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's lightly browned.
  4. Pour in the tomato puree, season with salt, and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with naan or rice.
