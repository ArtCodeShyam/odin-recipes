Tom Yum Soup

Description

Tom Yum Soup is a flavorful and aromatic Thai soup known for its spicy and sour taste, filled with fragrant herbs and shrimp.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 3 slices galangal or ginger
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chili paste (optional)
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 small tomato, cut into wedges
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring the broth to a boil. Add lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal or ginger, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Add shrimp, mushrooms, and tomato to the pot and cook until the shrimp turns pink.
  3. Stir in fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste (optional) for added spiciness.
  4. Simmer for a few more minutes and remove from heat.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
